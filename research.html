My research focuses on developing analyses of and tools for computational semantics, model evaluation, and multilingual corpus linguistics. Broadly, I’m currently working on: (1) meaning across languages, plus (2) model evaluation and interpretability. I am interested in developing and using formal semantic representations (such as Abstract Meaning Representation and Uniform Meaning Representation), as well as examining and modelling translated and multilingual language data.

ACNLP

My research lab is called ACNLP (Amherst College Natural Language Processing).
